作者简介Biography

感谢你的停留，我是同济大学海洋系的一名在读博士生，目前在法国索邦大学联合培养，本科毕业于南方科技大学海洋系。读博后于2022年在学校附近的画室开始系统学习油画，为之痴迷，并在画室、海洋学院及校图书馆开展绘画创作与作品展览。2025年留法（尼斯）期间在地中海边获得许多创作灵感，决定一边读博，一边开始自己的艺术生涯。

当真正热爱的事务出现时，我无法对此视而不见。谢谢你喜欢与支持，愿我们都能走向属于自己的热爱。

Thank you for stopping by. I am a PhD candidate in Oceanography at Tongji University, currently a joint doctoral student at Sorbonne University. I began systematic oil painting training in 2022 and have exhibited works in studios, academic spaces, and libraries. During my stay in Nice in 2025, the Mediterranean Sea inspired many creations, leading me to pursue an artistic career alongside my doctoral research.

When something I truly love appears, I simply can’t look away from it. Thank you for your love and support—may we all find our way toward what we truly cherish.

创作实践Artistic Practice

我在图书馆、画室与野外搜集素材，以油画与天然媒介进行实验与记录。创作里常见的是对海洋与季节变化的观察。

I collect material from the studio, library and outdoors, experimenting with oil paint and natural media. My practice often examines the sea and seasonal change.
